Introduction

This post explores the most common surnames across the Middle East through wordcloud visualisation. Outer boundaries of this region has many definitions, depending on classification purpose, classifying organization, historical period. Here, it includes selected countries of Western Asia (the European portion of Türkiye is shown), Southern Asia, and Northern Africa from UN M49, Standard Country or Area Codes for Statistical Use (Series M, No. 49) ⎋. Political boundaries shown according to their de facto status ⎋. Data came from Forebears.io ⎋.
