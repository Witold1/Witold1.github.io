Surnames of Balkan Peninsula

version: public ▪️ created: Mid-2024

Introduction

This post explores the most common surnames across the Balkan Peninsula through wordcloud visualisation. Region includes selected countries of Southern Europe, Eastern Europe, and Western Asia (the European portion of Türkiye, not shown here) from "UN M49, Standard Country or Area Codes for Statistical Use (Series M, No. 49)" ⎋. Political boundaries shown according to their "de facto" status ⎋. Data came from Forebears.io.
