Surnames of Iberian Peninsula

version: public ▪️ created: Mid-2024

Introduction

This post explores the most common surnames across the Iberian Peninsula through wordcloud visualisation. Region includes selected countries of Southern Europe from "UN M49, Standard Country or Area Codes for Statistical Use (Series M, No. 49)" ⎋. Political boundaries shown according to their "de facto" status ⎋. Data came from Forebears.io.
